#30DayMapChallenge
Day 11: Minimal Map

Data Source:
1. Rivers: https://www.hydrosheds.org/products/hydrorivers

Steps:
1. Load the data. Set symbology to Categorised and select ORD_CLAS for rivers by Classical Stream Order or ORD_STRA for Strahler Stream Order. For the former, smaller numbers should be darker while for the latter larger numbers should be darker.
